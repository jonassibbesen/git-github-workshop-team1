INGREDIENTS
FOR THE DRESSING
1/4 c. honey
1/4 c. freshly squeezed orange juice
Zest of 1 lemon

FOR THE SALAD
1 lb. strawberries, hulled and quartered
6 oz. blueberries
6 oz. raspberries
3 kiwis, peeled and sliced
1 orange, peeled and wedges cut in half
2 apples, peeled and chopped
1 mango, peeled and chopped
2 c. grapes
